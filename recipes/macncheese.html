Chicken Mac 'n' Cheese

Description

A go to family favorite made up of macaroni pasta and shredded chicken smothered in a rich cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g Cooked shredded chicken
  • 500g Macaroni Pasta
  • 40g Butter
  • 40g Plain Flour
  • 500g Cheese (Freshly Grated, dont use pre grated)
  • 500ml Semi-Skimmed Milk
  • 1tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 100ml Franks Buffalo Hot Sauce

Recipe

  1. First bring a pan of salted water to the boil and cook the pasta as per the packets instructions
  2. Drain the pasta and save about 100mls of the pasta water (this will come in handy later when adding the sauce)
  3. Pre-Heat your oven to 170 degrees Celsius
  4. Add the butter to a pan on a low heat and melt completely
  5. Add the flour to the butter and stir thoroughly until combined
  6. Once combined allow to cook for about 90 seconds
  7. Now slowly begin to add the milk in small amounts stirring constantly (it is important to go slow to ensure a smooth sauce)
  8. Once all the milk is added continue to stir and add in the Dijon Mustard and season with Salt and Pepper
  9. Now add 400g of your cheese in 100g (.Approx) increments stirring all the time until fully melted
  10. Take the sauce off the heat
  11. Add the pasta back into the pasta cooking pot and add a couple of table spoons of the pasta water, this should help break up any stuck pieces
  12. Add the chicken and sauce to the pasta and stir in (at this point also add the Buffalo sauce if you like it Hot!!!)
  13. Pour the mixture into a baking dish and sprinkle over the remaining cheese
  14. Place into the oven for 20 mins or until cheese on top is golden
  15. Enjoy!!!
