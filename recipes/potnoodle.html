Chicken Mac 'n' Cheese

Description

An easy on the go snack and a bachelors favorite

Ingredients

  • 1 Pot noodle, Flavour of your choice (or 2 if your really hungry)

Recipe

  1. Boil the kettle and fill up to the fill line as per the pots instructions
  2. Add the sauce sachet or don't if your boring and love bland food
  3. Leave to rest as per pot instructions
  4. Enjoy and spill all over your top
